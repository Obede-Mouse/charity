Services

Pharmaceutical Products

We offer a wide range of high-quality pharmaceutical products to support the healthcare industry in Juba and South Sudan at large. Our products are sourced from trusted manufacturers and comply with international standards.

Feminine Care

Our feminine care products are designed to provide comfort, hygiene, and protection for women. We prioritize the health and well-being of women in South Sudan by offering safe and reliable products.

Agriculture

Our agriculture services focus on promoting sustainable farming practices and providing farmers in Juba with access to quality seeds, fertilizers, and modern agricultural techniques. We assist in maximizing crop yields and improving food security.
